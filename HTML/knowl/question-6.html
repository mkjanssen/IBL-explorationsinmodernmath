Question 2.0.6. How can I get help in this class?

Given the inquiry-oriented nature of the course, your main avenue for help is to ask questions of others in your groups. If your group is stuck, I'll be more than happy to start you in the right direction, but will do my best not to give any answers away. If you are unable to resolve your questions in class, I welcome student hours appointments in my office (SB 1608). I prefer that you book an appointment ahead of time, but this is not required. If my door is open, just walk in! If it's not, you may knock, but I may ask that you make an appointment for another time.
