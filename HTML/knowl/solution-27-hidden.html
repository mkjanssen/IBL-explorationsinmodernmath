We may begin by supposing Corrie is a knave 1 . In that case, her statement is false, and there must be 0 or 2 knights. But if Corrie is a knave, there cannot be 2 knights, so there must be 0 knights, in which case Will's statement is false. However, this leads us to the conclusion that Corrie is a knight, yet we began by assuming that she is a knave.

This contradiction forces us to conclude that Corrie is a knight, and her statement implies that she is the only knight. Thus, Will is a knave.
