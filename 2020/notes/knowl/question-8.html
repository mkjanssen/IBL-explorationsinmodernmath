Question 2.0.8. What are the journals about? When are they due?

Each journal will look a little differently depending on our current topic. Sometimes you may be asked to read a short article or book chapter and react to it. Other times you may be asked to solve a few puzzles and discuss the process by which you solved them. Still other journals may look even more different; you'll find out when you open them.

You'll have at least a week to think about each journal. Journals are officially due some Fridays at 5pm. Unofficially, they may be turned in until midnight the next day. Journals are due:

  • January 17
  • January 24
  • January 31
  • February 14
  • February 28
  • March 20
  • March 27
  • April 3
  • April 17
  • May 1

Journals will be graded pass/no pass based on your ability to follow the given prompts.
