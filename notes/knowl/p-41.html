Throughout scripture, Christians are exhorted in various ways and to various activities regarding truth. We are told that knowledge of "the truth" will "set [us] free" 1 , Christ calls Himself "the way, the truth, and the life" 2 , and says that his mission is to testify to the truth 3 . Further, Paul exhorts believers to think on "whatever is true" 4 . But what is truth? Can mathematics help us better apprehend and understand it?
