Solving the Cube.

When you can consistently perform Cube Move 3.4.2, Cube Move 3.5.1, Cube Move 3.5.3, and Cube Move 3.6.1, you can use them to solve the Cube. You can use any strategy you want, but here is one to consider.

  1. Put the corners in the correct location.
  2. Put the edges in the correct location.
  3. Reorient any corners that need reorienting.
  4. Reorient any edges that need reorienting.
  5. Celebrate!
  6. Scramble the Cube and do it again.

Once you can consistently solve the Cube, make an appointment with Dr. Janssen to take your Rubik's Cube MFA. We'll spend all of next class working on solving our Cubes.
