Activity 4.4.6.
  1. Suppose you meet a native who you believe is a knight, and the native says, "There is gold on Smullyan Island." Do you go searching for gold? Explain.
  2. Suppose you meet a native who you believe is a knave, and the native says, "There is gold on Smullyan Island." Do you go searching for gold? Explain.
  3. Can any inhabitant of Smullyan Island ever say, "I am not a knight?" Explain.
  4. Suppose you meet a native who you believe is a knight, and the native says, "You will believe that I have a pot of gold." What do you believe? Explain.
  5. Suppose you meet a native who you believe is a knight, and the native says, "You will never believe that I have a pot of gold." What do you believe? Explain.
  6. Suppose you meet a native who you believe is a knight; explain why you believe whatever the native says.
