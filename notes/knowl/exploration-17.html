Exploration 5.3.4.

Again, assume we have six players: Amanda, Bob, Carla, Derek, Ellie, and Fred (two are spies). Assume further that Rebels always tell the truth and Spies always lie.

  • Round 1: Amanda and Derek go on the first mission; Two Success cards appear.
  • Round 2: At the start of Round 2, Ellie looks at Derek's card and says he is a Spy. Amanda, Ellie, and Fred go on the second mission. One Fail and two Success cards appear.
  • Round 3: At the start of Round 3, Derek looks at Carla's card and says she is a Spy. Carla, Bob, Amanda, and Fred go on the third mission. One Fail card and three Success cards appear.
  • Round 4: At the start of Round 4, Carla looks at Fred's card and says he is a Rebel. One of the Rebels pipes up that (s)he knows who everyone is!

Who are the Spies? Who are the Rebels?
