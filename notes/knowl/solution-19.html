Since I didn't use 15, I don't have to change anything.

The pattern described by the equations suggests a famous mathematical conjecture due to Goldbach: that every even whole number greater than or equal to 6 can be written as the sum of two (possibly equal) primes. Mathematicians have gathered a lot of evidence for this conjecture 1 , but until we have a conclusive proof, we won't know for sure if the pattern continues.
