Item.
Group work presentations will be graded on an M/N scale; if you have something productive to say, each member of your group will get an M, and if not, each will get an X. You may not revise presentations which are not assessable. There is no limit to the number of times your group can be selected to present, so you can accumulate the number of successful presentations in line with your goal by repeatedly volunteering to present.
