Investigation 5.1.5.

Consider the following system. Each citizen of Sudden Valley votes, and the votes are counted. The winner is the candidate with the smallest number of votes. This is known as minority rule.

  1. Suppose that the citizens of Sudden Valley vote, with 1000 voters voting for Michael and one (George, no longer a dictator) votes for Buster. Who wins under minority rule?
  2. Now suppose that George convinces 500 of the prospective Michael voters to switch and vote for Buster. Who wins in this case under minority rule?
  3. Does minority rule treat all voters equally? Does it treat all candidates equally? Explain.
  4. Under minority rule, is it beneficial or detrimental for a candidate to receive additional votes? Explain.
