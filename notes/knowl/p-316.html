This week, we will be diving further into exploring how discrete dynamical models give us the power to understand and make predictions about the Creation. We'll explore a basic predator/prey model and see how even relatively simple models of predator/prey interactions can behave qualitatively similarly to what we observe in the physical world. Then, we will explore a basic compartmental epidemiological model. We will make explicit connections to the ongoing efforts of public health officials and epidemiologists to "flatten the curve" regarding the spread of COVID-19.
